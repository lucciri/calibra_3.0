    Solutions for banking with ease
     

    You’re busy building a company. We’ve got services designed to help you accomplish more as you work to grow your business.

    SVB Edge
     

    Optimized for startups

    Everything pre-series A companies need to help with jump-starting your business.
    Free checking for your first three years¹, 4.33% annual percentage yield (APY)² on savings, and 2X unlimited reward points³ on card purchases.
    • A business checking account with no maintenance or transaction fees, free¹ online banking, unlimited incoming wires and ACH, unlimited outgoing wires including FX, bill payments and mobile deposits.
    • Connections to Quickbooks, Xero, Expensify and other authorized applications.
    • A startup money market account with a competitive APY of 4.33%².
    • A business credit card with 2x unlimited points³, no annual fee and no foreign transaction fees. 
    Learn more  

    SVB ScaleUp
     

    Optimized for startups and venture-funded

    Banking services for growing companies with an increased number of transactions. 

    Accelerate your business with a $500 monthly credit that can be applied to your service fees.4
    • A business checking account with the $50 monthly fee waived for the first year
    • Online banking including foreign exchange5, customized reporting and connections to authorized accounting applications. 
    • Wires - incoming and outgoing (USD domestic and international, FX).
    • Lockbox Services - including remote, wholesale and imaging.
    • SVB TransactGateway for payment automation through back-office accounting software or ERP.
    • Remote Deposit Capture for scanning and sending electronic images of checks directly to SVB from your desktop.
    • Fraud Control Services including Positive Pay with Payee Validation and ACH Blocks/Filters.

    Optimized for venture-funded and corporate banking

    Banking services for scaling companies with higher transaction volumes at any business stage.

    Standard pricing applies and service charges can be offset with an earnings credit based on your account balances. More details here.

    Access to a full suite of cash management services for companies with more complex banking needs.

    • Detailed monthly analysis statements let you easily monitor account activity

    More ways SVB can help you bank smarter – and more efficiently

     
    Our banking solutions come with a range of features that may help you streamline your business processes and get more efficient.

    Connect with a variety of popular business apps like QuickBooks, Xero, Expensify4 plus many others for better financial management.

    Sync your account with back-office accounting software or ERP solutions for more efficient payment automation.
